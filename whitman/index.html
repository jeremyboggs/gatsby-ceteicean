What think you I have taken my pen to record? Not the battle-ship,Some battleship1 perfect- model'd, majestic, that I saw to day arrive in the offing, under full sail, Nor the splendors of the pastAnother note2 day—nor the splendors of the night that envelopes me— Nor the glory and growth of the great citythe great city spread around me, But the two young men I saw to-day on the pier, parting the parting of dear friends. The one who remained to remain hung on the other's neck and passionately kissed him—while the one who departed to depart tightly prest the one who remained to remain in his arms. Some city.
